Our Story

A Legacy of Italian Craftsmanship

Since 1994, The Palazzo has stood as a beacon of culinary excellence in the heart of London. Founded by Chef Lorenzo Moretti, our establishment was born from a singular vision: to transport the authentic, aristocratic flavors of Northern Italy to discerning diners.


Every dish we serve is a testament to tradition, crafted with ingredients sourced directly from small-scale artisans across Piedmont, Lombardy, and Emilia-Romagna. We believe that fine dining is not merely about consumption, but about participating in a timeless ritual of elegance.
